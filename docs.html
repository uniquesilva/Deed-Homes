Selection Criteria

DEED targets properties that meet strict investment criteria:

  • Positive Cash Flow: Must generate rental income from day one
  • Growing Markets: Located in areas with increasing rental demand

  • Professional Management: Access to reliable property management services
  • Appreciation Potential: Markets with strong long-term growth prospects

FAQ

How often are buybacks executed?

Buybacks occur monthly using 50% of rental income from all properties.

Can I visit the properties?

Properties are managed by professional companies, but we provide regular updates and photos.

What happens if a property becomes vacant?

Professional property management handles tenant placement. Vacancy periods are factored into projections.

How is governance implemented?

Governance uses Snapshot for voting, with results implemented transparently by the team.
